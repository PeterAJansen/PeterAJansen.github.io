Extraction Schema (General Information)

Schema ID: extraction-schema-1846

Extraction Query: Extract any mentions of hidden drivers, pathway states, subtype structure, validation evidence, and therapeutic implications in oncogene-negative or driver-negative lung adenocarcinoma.

Extraction Schema (Details)

Field Name Type Description
driver_negative_definition str How the paper defines oncogene-negative or driver-negative LUAD. Include which canonical genes, pathways, or alteration classes were excluded, what assays were used, and what exactly counts as negative.
candidate_driver_pathway_or_mechanism str The main driver hypothesis proposed for the driver-negative tumors. Include whether the paper argues for rare genes, pathway activation state, tumor suppressor loss, proteomic activation, ligand dependence, epigenetic regulation, microenvironmental dependence, microbiome-related effects, lineage state, or another mechanism.
alteration_or_activation_mode str How the proposed driver operates. Examples: rare mutation, fusion, exon skipping, amplification, deletion, noncoding regulatory event, epigenetic silencing, outlier expression, protein loss, phosphoproteomic activation, ligand-receptor stimulation, or combinatorial co-loss of negative regulators.
implicated_gene_axis_or_pathway str The specific gene, gene set, ligand-receptor axis, or pathway implicated as functionally important. Examples: NF1/RASA1/PTEN loss, MAPK, PI3K/AKT/mTOR, SHP2, ERBB signaling, Hippo/YAP, oxidative stress, chromatin remodeling, cell-cycle program.
pathway_activation_evidence str Evidence that a pathway is activated or suppressed in the driver-negative tumors. Include markers such as pERK, pAKT, pS6, inferred kinase activity, expression signatures, IHC, or phosphoproteomic readouts, and note whether activation is genetically explained or unexplained.
driver_negative_subtype str Any explicit or implicit subtype within driver-negative LUAD proposed by the paper. Include subtype label, defining features, and whether the subtype is based on genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, immune context, metabolism, lineage state, histology, or integrated multi-omics.
clinical_or_exposure_context str Clinical, pathologic, or etiologic features linked to the driver-negative cases or subtype, such as smoking status, never-smoker status, age, sex, stage, mucinous histology, ancestry, prior treatment, prognosis, or recurrence.
cross_cancer_parallel str Any explicit comparison to other cancers with similar driver-negative biology, hidden driver logic, pathway dependence, lineage program, or therapeutic vulnerability.
therapeutic_vulnerability_or_implication str Any therapeutic implication linked to the candidate driver, pathway state, or subtype. Include drugs, inhibitor classes, immunotherapy relevance, synthetic lethal hypotheses, biomarker-linked treatment strategies, and whether evidence is preclinical or clinical.
validation_evidence_and_model_system str How the paper validates the proposed driver or subtype. Include model system and validation strength, such as independent cohort replication, cell lines, organoids, xenografts, GEMMs, knockdown or overexpression, rescue experiments, inhibitor sensitivity, or patient response.
driver_negative_scope_class str Normalize the paper's negative definition into a controlled category such as egfr_alk_only, egfr_kras_alk, broad_clinical_panel, rtk_ras_raf_axis, no_known_actionable_driver, not_formally_defined, or not_applicable. Extract what the paper actually operationalized, not an inferred broader meaning.
excluded_driver_genes_and_alteration_types str List the exact genes and alteration classes explicitly ruled out, preserving type detail such as hotspot mutation, non-hotspot mutation, exon skipping, amplification, fusion, CNV, IHC, FISH, WES, WGS, or RNA-seq.
negative_call_assay_stack str Extract the ordered assay sequence used to establish negativity, from initial clinical testing to rescue assays. Represent as a short ordered list such as ARMS_PCR -> FISH -> panel_NGS -> WGS -> RNA_seq.
negative_call_confidence str Assign low, medium, or high confidence for the negative call based on assay breadth, purity concerns, and whether broad DNA plus RNA methods were used. Lower confidence if the paper reports likely false negatives or limited-panel ascertainment.
rescued_hidden_driver_after_deeper_testing str Record whether initially negative cases were later reclassified by broader testing, using values such as none_reported, yes_specific_driver_found, mixed_some_rescued, or unresolved_after_broad_testing. Also briefly name the rescued event if present.
primary_hidden_driver_mechanism_class str Assign one dominant class from a controlled vocabulary: rare_coding_oncogene, kinase_fusion, ligand_fusion_or_ligand_presentation, exon_skipping_splicing_activation, copy_number_or_amplicon_driver, enhancer_hijacking_3d_genome, tumor_suppressor_co_loss, phosphoproteomic_pathway_state, lineage_plasticity_cell_state, microenvironment_ecosystem_state, metabolic_dependency_state, mutational_process_genomic_instability, technical_false_negative, or unresolved.
secondary_mechanism_classes str List additional mechanism classes if the paper argues for more than one biologic layer. Keep the primary class as the paper's main explanatory logic.
mechanism_requires_specific_genetic_lesion str Record yes, no, mixed, or unclear depending on whether the proposed explanation depends on a specific somatic lesion rather than a state or ecosystem.
mechanism_is_tumor_intrinsic_or_extrinsic str Classify the mechanism as tumor_intrinsic, microenvironment_extrinsic, mixed, mutational_process_level, or technical_artifact.
mechanism_biologic_level str Classify the dominant biologic level as dna, splicing_rna, chromatin_3d_genome, protein_phosphosite, lineage_state, microenvironment_ecosystem, or evolutionary_process.
causal_evidence_tier str Assign the strongest support level as one of tier_1_genomic_or_correlative_only, tier_2_expression_or_proteomic_association, tier_3_functional_cell_model, tier_4_in_vivo_model, or tier_5_human_clinical_response_or_multimodal_causal_support.
evidence_modalities_present str List all evidence modalities reported, such as recurrence_statistics, WGS, RNA_seq, phosphoproteomics, IHC, knockdown, rescue, transformation_assay, organoid, xenograft, GEMM, retrospective_clinical_response, or prospective_clinical_response.
therapeutic_actionability_evidence_tier str Assign none, inferential_only, preclinical_cell, preclinical_in_vivo, retrospective_clinical, or prospective_clinical for the therapy implication.
paper_claim_strength str Classify the authors' framing as descriptive, hypothesis_generating, mechanistic, biomarker_proposal, or clinically_actionable.

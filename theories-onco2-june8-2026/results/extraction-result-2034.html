The fusion logic is also compared to EML4-ALK and KIF5B-ALK oncokinase fusions in lung cancer and other solid-tumor fusion drivers.
therapeutic_vulnerability_or_implication The paper proposes KIF5B-RET as a promising molecular target for personalized diagnosis and treatment of lung cancer and suggests that specific agents targeting KIF5B-RET would enable more advanced therapeutic strategies.
